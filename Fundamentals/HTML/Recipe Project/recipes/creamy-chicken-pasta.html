Creamy Chicken Pasta

Description

This dish has become a favourite in our house for the sticky, sweet taste, flavour-filled bites and speed to make. It is a hit for the adults and young children, down to my middle one at age 4! Sadly, it's frowned upon to feed a 3 month old solid food yet, but will be trying her with this dish as soon as we can.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Honey
  • Chicken Breast

Steps

  1. Mix flour and herbs and set aside in their own bowl
  2. Crack and whisk egg and set aside in it's own bowl
  3. Cut chicken into small bitesized chunks
